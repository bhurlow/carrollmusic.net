Songs from their first EP, Needs premiered on Tiny Mix Tapes and have been a mainstay on radio stations and playlists across the country. Carroll’s psychedelic-shoegazey-pop led to their being named Best New Band by Minneapolis’ two biggest arts publications- City Pages and Vita.mn. This past March, Carroll played several showcases at SXSW followed by a 2-week tour of the East Coast, Midwest, and Montreal. In the past year the band has opened for and played with the likes of Dr. Dog, Electric Six, Pure Bathing Culture, Nightlands, Gardens & Villa, Le1f, and many more. Carroll just cut their debut full-length with producer/ engineer Jon Low (The National, Twin Sister, Local Natives) in Philadelphia.
